About Me

My name is Jonathan Yee and I am a photographer whose portfolio is filled with a wide variety of subjects including (1) animals, (2) food and (3) people. My professional experience in the industry began as a hobby, stemming from my eternal love for photography and the arts. Previous to my current work, I have enjoyed taking photos of friends, landscapes and nature. One specific passion of focus would be photographing the curious on goings of the domesticated pet.

Growing up on the west coast surrounded by multi-cultural influences, I always intended to attend a 4 year University program and was lucky enough to be accepted to one of the prestigious California, UC schools. As a UC Riverside Business Marketing Major, I was afforded the opportunity to take photos of on campus life, a unique natural environment of the school's surrounding orange groves and many graduation ceremonies. I have always found the excitement from on campus events as an amazing opportunity to capture in photos.

As your future photographer, I intend to hold initial meetings with the client to get a grasp of the event I am contracted to shoot. Getting close with subjects and obtaining context will allow me to capture signficant moments effectively. Your satisfaction is my focus and guarantee.
